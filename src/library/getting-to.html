The Library is in the Media Centre in the West Basement of Beit Quad, which is the main student union building on the South Kensington campus. There are the occasional sign to us around the Quad (called 'Science Fiction and Fantasy Library'). The fastest way to get to us, however, is.

  • Enter Beit Quad under the archway
  • Take the second left, past the bike racks
  • Enter the west basement through the doors ahead of you (shown right)
  • Go down the stairs, and turn right
  • Pass through the internal doors, and the library is the second door on your right
