Budget

  • Membership target:
  • Last year, the target was 90 members.
  • Currently we’re on 74 members (82% of the target).
  • We haven’t met the target for the last few years.
  • We have 8 life members, but they’re not counted toward the target.
  • Could consider lowering the target to 80 to 85.
  • Probably more realistic, consistent number of members.
  • Will most likely hit 80 members this year.
  • Some people are here but haven’t yet bought membership.
  • Can also attract more members through bar nights.
  • Putting budget toward cinema tickets rather than snacks for nights in might attract more members, but snacks are cheaper.
  • Will also publicise the difference in ticket price for Picocon.
  • Cinema tickets:
  • Normally £11 in London, can’t subsidise by 50%.
  • Could decide on a blanket subsidy of $3, or a particular percentage of the ticket price, or a limit on number of subsidised tickets.
  • The first two options could be advertised.
  • A lot of people come in first term, and this year the subsidy was a lot, so could be a good idea to limit numbers.
  • A solid £3 would be easier to budget for, more appealing to people (could still round it so easier to collect money).
  • Could consider running a campaign – would get funding if we have a good idea.
  • Could consider looking into a tour, with a tour budget.
