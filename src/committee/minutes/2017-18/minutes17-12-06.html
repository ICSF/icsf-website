Re-shelving

  • This term??? No. Suggestion has been made that it should take place a week after the start of next term.
  • Last time we did stock check it took 6 people to do it. We will need many people this time because putting in alphabetical order will take a lot of time. People will be in the aisles and others looking into boxes. We could advertise it but people won’t be generally bothered.
  • Tall people needed
  • Henry doesn’t want to be alone so suggestion is that we do it over one weekend.
  • ‘New year, new library’ thing. We will bring cakes, biscuits, drinks, watch favourite movies etc
  • We can’t move books too soon. We were asked to come up with ideas how to additionally waterproof valuables in the library. Estates will take care of the ceiling.
    • Bad ideas examples – shower curtains over shelves (flammable, looks less pretty, less professional); every book put in a plastic bag.
    • Safety rules forbid us to attach things to the ceiling above books.
    • We are open to any sensible ideas. E.g. carpet is easier to replace than books, therefore we need to come up with anything soon.
    • Getting new cabinets would be expensive and would require lots of time to do so.
    • Best idea so far: put a waterproof hat on the shelves
