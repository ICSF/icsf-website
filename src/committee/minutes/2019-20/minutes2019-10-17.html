Request List

  • Is it okay if I decide not to get some things for the library from the request list or are we obliged to get everything from there?
    • It depends on the reason why you’re not getting it.
    • Examples:
      • Can’t find it, and too expensive – are good reasons
      • I hate it – is not.
    • You’re not expected to get everything, aim for 70% of the list – if you can’t decide whether or not to get something feel free to ask committee.
  • Also, some things are not even sci fi or fantasy.
    • Our remit is fantasy, scifi, horror, comics, and a few other topics so get things that fall under that.
    • There is the Italian Job Clause (which was used in order to add the Italian job to the library), where an item is outstandingly good can be added to the library despite it not fitting into our remit. This can be librarian’s choice, or you can query the committee.

Roku / TV

  • Asked Tim about the weird folders Sequoia found in the menu selection on the TV; committee believe it’s a thing from Physics Dept. (where the TV came from).

  • Roku is set up and working.
  • How to use:
    • Roku needs to be plugged into the mains via a ‘mains USB adaptor’ (E.g. Phone charger plug with USB port), rather than the TV USB port.
    • The TV's source must be set to HDMI 2.
    • The Roku has its own remote; it's small and black with purple accents.
  • Only committee can decide on what channels to add.
    • There is a pin that needs to be entered to add a channel and currently only the Tech-priest knows it.
  • Netflix needs to be signed into.
  • Prime is signed in on Tim’s account.
  • There are other channels available (like BBC iPlayer, and YouTube) that need to be looked at.
